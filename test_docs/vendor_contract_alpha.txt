VENDOR SERVICES AGREEMENT
Contract Reference: VSA-2024-0091
Date: 1 January 2024

PARTIES
This agreement is entered into between:
- Alpha Construction LLC, a company registered in Dubai, UAE (hereinafter "Vendor")
- Gulf Properties FZE, a free zone entity registered in JAFZA (hereinafter "Client")

1. SCOPE OF WORK
The Vendor agrees to supply and install building materials for the construction of residential
properties on behalf of UAE Nationals under the FTA VAT Refund Scheme. All materials
supplied must qualify as incorporated building materials under Federal Decree-Law No. 8 of 2017.

2. VAT AND TAXATION
2.1 All invoices issued by the Vendor shall apply VAT at the standard rate of 5% in accordance
    with UAE VAT Law.
2.2 The Vendor shall issue simplified tax invoices for all transactions below AED 10,000.
2.3 The Vendor confirms its Tax Registration Number (TRN) is active and valid.
2.4 VAT refund claims related to this contract must be submitted within 6 months of project
    completion.

3. PAYMENT TERMS
3.1 The Client shall make payment within 45 days of receiving a valid tax invoice.
3.2 All payments shall be processed on the 15th of each month regardless of invoice date.
3.3 Retention of 10% shall be held by the Client until project completion certificate is issued.
3.4 Retention payment shall be released within 90 days of the completion certificate.

4. ELIGIBLE MATERIALS
4.1 The following items are covered under this agreement and eligible for VAT recovery:
    - Structural materials (cement, steel, bricks)
    - Electrical wiring and fixed lighting fixtures
    - Plumbing and sanitary fittings permanently fixed to the structure
    - Flooring and wall tiles
    - Doors and windows
4.2 The following items are explicitly INCLUDED in this contract and eligible for VAT recovery:
    - Swimming pools and landscaping features
    - Furniture and electrical appliances
    - Decorative items and artwork

5. DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS
5.1 The Vendor shall provide full tax invoices for all transactions, including those below AED 10,000.
5.2 Invoices must include the property owner's Emirates ID number.
5.3 Bank payment confirmation is acceptable as proof of payment.
5.4 The Vendor is not required to include the owner's name on tax invoices.

6. OBLIGATIONS OF THE VENDOR
6.1 The Vendor must deliver all materials within 30 days of purchase order confirmation.
6.2 The Vendor must obtain a completion certificate from the municipality within 60 days of
    project handover.
6.3 The Vendor may subcontract work without prior written approval from the Client.
6.4 The Vendor must maintain project records for a minimum of 3 years.

7. OBLIGATIONS OF THE CLIENT
7.1 The Client must submit the VAT refund claim on behalf of property owners within 6 months
    of project completion.
7.2 The Client is responsible for verifying that all properties qualify under the FTA scheme.
7.3 The Client must notify the Vendor of any FTA audit within 5 business days.
7.4 In the event the building is converted to commercial use, the Client must repay the VAT
    refund to the FTA and may seek recovery from the Vendor.

8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
8.1 All disputes shall be resolved through arbitration under DIAC rules within Dubai.
8.2 Governing law: Laws of the UAE.

9. TERM
This agreement is valid from 1 January 2024 to 31 December 2024, with automatic renewal
unless terminated with 30 days written notice.
